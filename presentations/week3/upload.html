Minimalism at its Finest

Minimalism is the art of creating a website that is simple, pleasing to the eye, and free of clutter. It should look clean, professional, and catches people's attention.

You should always consider colours, fonts and graphics as the basis for your minimalist website.

Many believe that minimalism is the way to go. It is common in professional institutions as well as trendy websites. Some people live by the idea of minimalism and would die for the cause.

"I love minimalist websites. They are my pride and joy. I genuinely cannot look at websites that have more than two fonts; I think it's immoral." - Doug Johnson

Karen Little herself has even established the AMW, a group that is pushing for minimalist website standards such as including limitations on typefaces and dual colour schemes.
